IntelliLaw — LAN Access Instructions
======================================

IntelliLaw runs a local server on your PC that other devices on the
same network can access via a web browser.

1. Find your PC's IP address:
   - Open Command Prompt and run: ipconfig
   - Look for "IPv4 Address" under your active network adapter
     e.g. 192.168.1.100

2. On any device on the same Wi-Fi/LAN, open a browser and go to:
   http://<your-IP-address>:8000
   e.g. http://192.168.1.100:8000

3. Log in with your IntelliLaw credentials.

Note: The firewall on this PC must allow port 8000.
      IntelliLaw installer configures this automatically.
